Support the Supporters, both Academic and Informal


Academic Stakeholders

  1. Direct access to an interface which enables immediate content change and display.

    This data informs the bot, and updates important files. No need to go through a webmaster

  2. Use Keywords

    The use of keywords help the AI and routers find content keyword-labeled by the stakeholder. This, in turn, helps getting the right content to the student student, and aids in reducing the need for the student to learn jargon before he can find help.

Informal Support, such as family and friends

  1. Guest Accounts

    As much as possible, remove the need to login in order to find information needed to register for class, or finding campus services. Doing so allows those who support the student, such as family, friends, care-givers and caseworkers to participate in the process without the burden of taking time off from work to help the student.

Platform Initiative: Support Academic Staff
